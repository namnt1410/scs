About real PROTEIN sequences



*PROTEIN sequences are obtained from Swiss-Prot Release 45.5 of 04-Jan-2005. http://us.expasy.org/sprot.

Sequences are randomly selected from different protein sequences.
Seuqences datasets are truncated to specific length(K) and number(N) of sequences.

Each of the settings have 10 datasets, and dataset number i is named PROTEIN_NXXX_KXXX_i.
There are 60 sequences datasets in total.
